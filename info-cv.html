About

Based in Salt Lake City, Utah, Jake strives to bring generative elements into all his art & design work. He is passionate about interaction & installation, & how code & technology can be leveraged to elevate it. He also has a wide array of skills & interests in art, visual graphics, visual strategy, prototyping, & design research. He is currently a holder of a Bachelor of Science in Multi-Disciplinary Design from the University of Utah.
