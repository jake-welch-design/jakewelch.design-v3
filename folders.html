Project description

FOLDERS is a prototype open-source website targeted towards designers for collecting, finding, & sharing resources

Links

↳ Full interactive prototype on Figma

Developed during the fall 2022 Multi-Disciplinary Design junior studio in the College of Architecture & Planning at the University of Utah, FOLDERS was designed with the idea of creating an "online commons", a place where designers can openly & freely collaborate to collect, find, & share resources. As designers we have to keep track of many tools & inspirations – this tool is designed to not only make it easier, but to encourage a community-focused approach in working with other designers.

Landing page

Profile tab

The website follows a simple structure of three main tabs, all accessible from the landing page & from a drop down menu.

The ‘Profile’ tab is the hub for all resources saved by the user. Through folders & sub-folders, links can easily be saved, organized, accessed, & shared with others. When saving a link, users add tags which inform the algorithm for the ‘Explore’ tab. Resources can also be shared directly within Folders to groups & other users.

Explore tab

Collab tab

The ‘Explore’ tab contains the feed of all algorithmically recommended resources as well as the folders of other users similar to those of the users. Links & folders can be quickly added to the user’s own profile by clicking the blue ‘plus’ icon.

The ‘Collab’ tab is where users can find & join groups which cater to their interests. By joining these new communities, users are encouraged to work with each other to strengthen each other in design. Links from the users’ profile can be quickly shared here, & resources can be added to their profile by clicking the blue ‘plus’ icon which will automatically appear next to links.
