Founded by Studio Dumbar/DEPT® and Global, DEMO Festival aims to showcase the worlds best motion design by displaying the works of designers from all across the globe on more than 5000 screens across the Netherlands, including train stations, metros, shopping centers, Schiphol Airport, Eindhoven Airport and Rotterdam The Hague Airport, on billboards along major highways, and in the streets. This piece was submitted & selected to be shown as part of the event.
